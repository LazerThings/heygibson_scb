Super Construct World

Super Construct World is the first game in the Super Construct Bros. series, a Mario fangame series built in the fanmade level editor Super Mario Construct.

There are 8 worlds, 2 of them being special. You can play through in intended order or freely across the levels. There are 30 levels in total.

It also has some inside jokes...
